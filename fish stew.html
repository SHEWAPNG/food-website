Fish Stew Recipe

Ingredients

  • Fish (Any fish of your choice)
  • Palm oil or Vegetable Oil
  • Blended pepper (tomatoes, bell peppers, and onions)
  • 1 Maggi cube
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Seasonings (like curry and thyme)

Instructions

1. Prepare the fish by cleaning and cutting it into pieces.

2. Add some salt to it after washing

3. Blend your peppers (tomatoes, bell peppers, and onions) to make a smooth paste.

Frying your fish

4. Heat palm oil in a pot

5. Put your fish inside the pot that that contains the heated palm oil

6. After Frying it to your taste, Remove the fish from the pot and put it inside a plate .

Cooking Your Fish Stew

7. Add some chopped onions into the heated palm oil or vegetable oil .

8.Pour the blended pepper inside the pot that contains the heated palm oil or vegetable that was used to fry the fish earlier.

9. Add your fried fish

10. Add some water to the pot, followed by the Maggi cube, salt, and seasonings like curry and thyme.

11. Allow the stew to cook well until the fish is fully cooked and the flavors meld together.

12. Serve hot with rice or plantains.
