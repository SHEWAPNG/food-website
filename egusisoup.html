Traditional Village-Style Egusi Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Egusi seeds (ground)
  • 1/2 cup of palm oil
  • 1/4 cup of locust beans (iru)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 smoked fish (washed and deboned)
  • 1/2 cup of spinach or pumpkin leaves (or local vegetables)
  • 1 tablespoon ground crayfish
  • 1 Maggi cube
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 2 cups of water or meat stock

Procedure

  1. Heat the palm oil in a pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the locust beans and chopped onions. Fry for about 2-3 minutes .
  3. Add some blended pepper
  4. Add the ground Egusi seeds into the oil mixture and stir well. Allow the Egusi to fry for a few minutes, stirring occasionally, until it slightly thickens.
  5. Pour in the water or meat stock and stir. Let the mixture cook for about 10 minutes until the Egusi is fully cooked and thickens further.
  6. Add the smoked fish, ground crayfish, Maggi cube, and salt. Stir and let it simmer for another 5-7 minutes to absorb the flavors.
  7. Finally, add the spinach or pumpkin leaves and stir. Allow to cook for 3-5 more minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  8. Serve hot with pounded yam, fufu, or your preferred side.
