Rice and Beans with Fish Stew Recipe

Ingredients

  • Desired cup of beans
  • Desired cup of rice
  • Fish (your choice)
  • Palm oil
  • Blended pepper (tomatoes, bell peppers, and onions)
  • 1 Maggi cube
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Seasonings (like curry and thyme)

Instructions

1. Cook the beans in water until they are a little softer.

2. Add the rice to the pot with the beans, and continue cooking until the rice is fully cooked.

3. While the rice and beans are cooking, prepare the fish stew:

4. Heat palm oil in a separate pot.

5. Add the chopped onions and fry until golden brown.

6. Add the fish and let it fry for a few minutes.

7. Blend your peppers (tomatoes, bell peppers, and onions) to make a smooth paste.

8. Pour the blended pepper into the pot with the fried fish.

9. Add some water to the pot, followed by the Maggi cube, salt, and seasonings like curry and thyme.

10. Allow the stew to cook well until the fish is fully cooked and the flavors meld together.

11. Serve the rice and beans hot, topped with the fish stew.
